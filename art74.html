Digital Art

Typologies Assignment using AI

+

I generated a digital art image of birds cuddling in a nest in the branches of a tree. I made this using AI for my first project in Art 74. Making this image was a chance to step outside my comfort zone and try something new, even though I don't plan to keep working in this style.

Digital Art

Pinterest Painting

+

This abstract artwork was made by combining 13 images, such as mountains, a space shuttle, a parakeet, pine branches, a sports car, and lightning storms. I changed these images using a water technique to create a stained-glass effect. The piece was not carefully planned; I was just experimenting to see what would happen.

Digital Art

Sticker project using Still Life Vector Drawing

+

I designed a sticker inspired by the "Diary of a Wimpy Kid" series, using the purple cover, spiral binding, loose-leaf paper, and hand-drawn lettering from the books. In one photo, the sticker is on a golden background. In another, it sits next to a handmade cardboard sculpture, which makes the smaller design stand out against the larger, textured object.

Digital Art

Creative Coding Main Project

+

A robotic figure coded from simple geometric shapes, with a quiet personality, its bold, deep blue head, red eye, and extensions shaped like triangles topped with gray spheres. A charcoal mouth is placed beneath its red eye, and a segmented steel base is used to ground the figure.
