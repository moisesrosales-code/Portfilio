Widow's view

A Charcoal and Pencil Art Piece for Art 24. Widow's view is a diptych of two paintings depicting the same scene from different angles. The art piece depicts a small figure standing on a balcony, gazing out at the hills and mountains.

The piece is a diptych, created in charcoal and pencil, depicting a figure looking out at distant hills and mountains from a balcony on a tower. The different textural techniques used on each piece give everything a unique identity, while the light and dark also enhance a sense of fascination.
