Random Flag

Random abstract flag piece.

The random flag is a piece I made in my late teens, a time when I was exploring abstract creativity. Made with paint and tape, it was and still is the first art piece I adore, a creation where I felt I poured in pure creativity, and I loved it. I gave it for display at an exhibition. I could have set a price to sell it, but I wouldn't let something I love be sold, so I just let it be watched. When the event was over, I took it back home, and it's still there to this day.
