Ball's Dream

A short video study focused on movement, atmosphere, and observation.

For this project, I made a video featuring the same tennis ball, presented in a dream-like style. Most of the video shows the ball in a park, sliding down slides, and rolling inside something like a bucket. I focused more on the mood and movement than on telling a clear story.

I used techniques from earlier projects and worked with both Adobe After Effects and Adobe Premiere Pro. I made the echo effect last longer and continued using music to enhance the dream-like feeling. Using these tools together helped me make smoother transitions and a more unified mood.

Overall, I liked how this project turned out because it brought together ideas and skills I have learned. It was a good way to show my progress with something that shows both my experiments and my own style.
