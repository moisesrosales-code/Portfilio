Exercise 3

In this sketch I started to work with custom functions and the tranlation functions.

For this assignment, I made a bird because I thought it would be a fun project with lots of parts: body, head, wings, beak, and feet. I used two functions to create different birds. One has a bow, one bounces off the walls, another wraps around the screen, and two of them spin and grow. Each bird is either red, blue, green, or yellow.
After some practice, making the birds bounce and wrap around the screen got easier. The hardest part was lining up each piece in the center, since even a small mistake made the bird look off. This project helped me learn how to break images into parts and use transforms to put them in the right place.
