A Tennis Ball Enjoying Life

A short video study focused on movement, atmosphere, and observation.

For this project, I made a short story about a tennis ball having the time of its life as it takes in the scenery, explores new places, and moves through buildings. I gave the tennis ball a personality and sense of curiosity by showing how it moves and interacts with its surroundings. To match the playful and adventurous mood, I added music and background sound effects.

I used Adobe After Effects for this project, especially for rotoscoping. I used it to isolate the tennis ball and add an echo effect in one shot, which made its movement stand out more. Once I finished the impact, I imported the clip into Adobe Premiere Pro to assemble the final edit and refine the timing and sound.

Switching between After Effects and Premiere Pro taught me how different programs can work together within a single workflow. This project helped me try out new effects while keeping the story simple and easy to follow.
