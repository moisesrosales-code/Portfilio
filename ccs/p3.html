Sticker project using Still Life Vector Drawing

Diary of a Wimpy Kid book sticker.

I designed a sticker inspired by the "Diary of a Wimpy Kid" series, using the purple cover, spiral binding, loose-leaf paper, and hand-drawn lettering from the books. I used Adobe Illustrator to create the sticker design. In one photo, the sticker is on a golden background. In another, it sits next to a handmade cardboard sculpture, which makes the smaller design stand out against the larger, textured object.
