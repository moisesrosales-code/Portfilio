Random Flag

Random abstract flag piece I made from high school.

The random flag is a piece I made in high school for an assignment that asked us to create something abstract. This is what I came up with. Made with paint and tape, it was and still is the first art piece I adore, a creation of mine where I felt I gave it my pure creativity, and I loved it. A piece I gave for the school's art exhibition. I could have set a price to sell it, but I wouldn't let something I love be sold, so I just let it be watched. When the event was over, I took it back home, and it's still there to this day.
