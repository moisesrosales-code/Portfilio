Taking in Nature

A short video study focused on movement, atmosphere, and observation.

For my first project in ART 75, I picked scenery shots that show calm, natural places. I looked for footage that matched the peaceful mood I wanted, with forests and hills. To add to the feeling, I used background sounds like wind and birds. I wanted the visuals and audio to work well together, so I kept things simple and avoided too many effects or quick cuts.

I made the whole project in Adobe Premiere Pro, using simple but effective editing. I tried out different transitions to keep the shots connected and the flow smooth. I also adjusted the colors to make the scenery look more lively but still natural, which helped set the mood.

This project made me more confident using Adobe Premiere Pro and showed me how small edits can really change the final result. I now feel better about using transitions, sound, and color changes to create a calm and immersive mood.
